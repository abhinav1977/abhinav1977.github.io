📖 About the Application

MobileTrans-Phone-Transfer helps you move data between mobile devices on Windows 11 and 10. The application simplifies the process of changing phones. You connect your devices to your computer and transfer contacts, messages, photos, and videos with a few clicks. This tool supports various phone brands and operating systems.

⚙️ System Requirements

Your computer must meet these basic needs to run the software:

  • Operating System: Windows 10 or Windows 11 (64-bit).
  • Processor: 1 GHz or faster.
  • Memory: At least 2 GB of RAM.
  • Storage: 500 MB of free disk space.
  • Connection: USB cables for your mobile devices.

⬇️ How to Download and Install

Follow these steps to set up the software on your Windows computer.

  1. Visit the official download page to obtain the latest installer.
  2. Click the download link to save the installer file to your computer.
  3. Locate the file in your downloads folder.
  4. Double-click the file to start the installation.
  5. Follow the prompts on your screen. Leave the settings at their defaults for the best experience.
  6. Click Finish when the setup completes.

🚀 Getting Started

Once you finish the installation, you can connect your hardware to begin the transfer.

  1. Launch the application from your desktop shortcut.
  2. Connect your source phone and your destination phone to your computer using USB cables.
  3. The application detects your devices automatically.
  4. Ensure the application shows the correct source phone on the left and the destination phone on the right.
  5. Click the Swap button if the order is incorrect.
  6. Select the types of files you wish to move.
  7. Click the Start button to begin the transfer.

Keep your phones connected until the progress bar reaches one hundred percent.

💡 Common Questions

What types of data can I transfer? You can move contacts, text messages, call logs, calendar events, photos, music, and videos.

Does phone brand matter? No. The software moves data between iPhones and Android phones interchangeably.

Will my data stay on the old phone? Yes. This process copies your data. It does not delete anything from the source device.

What if the computer does not see my phone? Check your USB cable first. Use the original cable if possible. Ensure your phone settings allow data transfer when connected to a computer.

🛡️ Security and Privacy

The application runs locally on your computer. Your data travels directly between your devices through your local USB connection. The software does not upload your personal files to the internet. This ensures that your private information stays under your control throughout the entire transfer process.
